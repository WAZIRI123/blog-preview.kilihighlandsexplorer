A Promise Fulfilled on Kilimanjaro: A Father-Son Climb for Cancer Awareness

Mount Kilimanjaro - Africa's highest peak and the backdrop for an inspiring journey of hope

At Kili Highlands Explorer, every climb tells a story of courage, connection, and purpose. Earlier this year, in collaboration with Cancer Support, we had the honour of hosting an unforgettable expedition for a father and son whose journey up Mount Kilimanjaro went far beyond the summit.

This was more than a mountain climb; it was a promise kept, a message of hope, and a powerful stand for men's health awareness.

A Journey Born from a Promise

In 2021, a father and his son, Kane, dreamed of marking the end of Kane's school years with a great adventure: climbing Mount Kilimanjaro together. But as the world paused during the pandemic, their plans were put on hold.

They explored Namibia instead, but Kilimanjaro remained in their hearts, a challenge waiting to be met.

Then, in 2023, life took an unexpected turn when the father was diagnosed with prostate cancer. Supported by the dedicated team at Switzerland Cancer Support, he faced the battle head on. Following successful treatment and recovery, he made a new promise not only to finally climb Kilimanjaro with his son but to dedicate their ascent to raising awareness for men's health.

Why They Climbed

This climb was about more than reaching Africa's highest peak it was about keeping a promise, celebrating recovery, and inspiring others to take control of their health.

A well deserved break during Father and Son's Mount Kilimanjaro adventure

Their goals were simple yet profound:

  • To honour a promise between a father and son
  • To raise awareness for early men's health screening
  • To celebrate survival and the strength of family bonds
  • To inspire others to pursue their dreams, no matter the obstacles

Every step up the mountain became a step toward awareness an expression of gratitude and hope.

Training and Preparing for the Mount Kilimanjaro Climb

As preparations began, the father trained in Valencia, Spain, while Kane trained in the mountains around Geneva. Despite the distance, they shared a common rhythm early morning runs, weekend hikes, strength workouts, and long stair climbs with weighted packs.

Each training session brought them closer not only to the summit but to each other, united by purpose and determination.

The Climb: Lemosho Route with Kili Highlands Explorer

In October, they embarked on their long awaited adventure an eight day Lemosho Route expedition with Kili Highlands Explorer, renowned for its scenic beauty and excellent acclimatization profile.

From the moment they set foot in the lush rainforest to the final push toward Uhuru Peak at 5,895 meters, our expert guides were there ensuring safety, motivation, and a seamless journey through the mountain's breathtaking landscapes:

Father and Son with the incredible KILI HIGHLANDS EXPLORER team during they Mount Kilimanjaro climbing adventure.

This Route (Lemosho Route) Traverses Kilimanjaro's Four Dramatic Climatic Zones

  • Rainforest Zone: Alive with birdsong and colobus monkeys swinging above the canopy.
  • Moorlands: Expansive views, volcanic boulders, and shifting clouds that seemed to float at arm's length.
  • Alpine Desert: Harsh, rocky terrain that tested endurance and spirit.
  • Arctic Zone: An icy world above 5,000 meters where every breath became a triumph.

More Than a Mountain

For them, reaching the summit was not just about standing on top of Africa it was about standing for something greater.

Too many men delay health screenings or ignore early warning signs. This climb was their message to the world: get tested , stay aware, and never take your health for granted.

As dawn broke over Mawenzi Peak during their final ascent to Uhuru Peak, they carried with them not only their gear but the hopes of countless others fighting their own battles.
